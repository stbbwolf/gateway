About us

Gateway between China & the UAE

Truss Gateway is the first professional service provider in the UAE dedicated to bridging China-UAE collaborations, in the background of the join initiative of the two nations to restore silk road. We work closely with government, capital and enterprises in both nations to bridge information gap and exchange investment and business opportunities.

Truss Gateway provides one-stop service covering investment & management consulting, marketing, public & government relations, and trade sourcing. We also invest and operate in businesses to migrate Chinese technologies and products to UAE market.

We work closely with government and industrial organizations to foster collaboration between enterprises and academic institutions, and between industrial park in both nations.

Business & Investment

Businessand Investment

Truss Gateway actively seeks opportunities to bring Chinese technology and products into UAE market, and collaborate with UAE government entities and enterprises explore and operate businesses in the UAE.

Truss also invests and operates in introducing Chinese products and technology into the UAE. We are operating digital payment services in the UAE, with technology introduced from China. We connect Chinese up-stream navigation technology with UAE down-stream smart navigation big data platform to operate a joint in-door navigation business in the UAE. We are also working towards introducing Chinese tax management system for UAE government.

Real Estate

Real Estate

Truss Gateway works with sovereign and family funds to seek real estate projects in China based on required project profiles. We also help Chinese capital to explore investment opportunities in UAE real estate market.

Truss is partnering with an Abu Dhabi family fund to operate a real estate development business in the UAE, targeting both new and stressed development projects. We are now seeking a Chinese development / management partner to setup a joint venture in the UAE.

Manufacturing & Industrial Parks

Exploring Industrial Parks in theUAE

Truss Gateway is working with Abu Dhabi Department of Economic Development to bring Chinese manufacturers to operate in the UAE. Truss represents Chinese enterprises to negotiate for favorable policies for Chinese companies to setup presence in UAE industrial parks. We are also working with various UAE universities and industrial park to explore collaborations with Chinese counterparts.

UAE is an ideal location for Chinese manufacturing and trading business. The labor and operational costs are at par compared to those in China, but energy costs and taxes are significantly lower then China. This also represents a great opportunities for Chinese exporters to the US given the current China-US trade situations.
